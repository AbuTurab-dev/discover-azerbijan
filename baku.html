Discover Baku

Where ancient history meets modern architecture in Azerbaijan's vibrant capital

Baku: The City of Contrasts

Azerbaijan's capital Baku is a fascinating blend of old and new, where medieval walls stand in the shadow of futuristic skyscrapers. The city beautifully combines its rich history with modern development, offering visitors a unique urban experience.

From the UNESCO-listed Old City (Icherisheher) to the iconic Flame Towers, Baku showcases Azerbaijan's journey from ancient crossroads to contemporary metropolis. The city's location on the Caspian Sea adds to its charm, with the Baku Boulevard providing stunning waterfront views.

Top Attractions in Baku

Must-see landmarks and experiences in Azerbaijan's capital

Old City (Icherisheher)

This UNESCO World Heritage site is the historic heart of Baku, dating back to the 12th century. Explore the narrow streets, visit the Maiden Tower, and discover ancient mosques and bathhouses.

Flame Towers

Baku's most iconic modern landmark, these three skyscrapers are shaped like flames and feature a spectacular LED light show at night. The towers house a hotel, apartments and offices.

Baku Boulevard

This scenic promenade along the Caspian Sea stretches for several kilometers, offering beautiful views, cafes, amusement rides, and the iconic Baku Eye Ferris wheel.
