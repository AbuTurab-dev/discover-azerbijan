Tourist Visa to Azerbaijan

Everything you need to know about obtaining a visa for your trip to Azerbaijan

Visa Types

Tourist Visa

Single or multiple entry visa valid for up to 30 days stay.

E-Visa

Electronic visa obtained online before travel, processing time 3 working days.

Visa on Arrival

Available at selected airports for certain nationalities.

Application Process

  1. Check eligibility: Verify if you need a visa based on your nationality
  2. Choose visa type: Select between e-visa or consular visa
  3. Prepare documents: Passport, photo, travel itinerary, etc.
  4. Submit application: Online for e-visa or at embassy/consulate
  5. Pay visa fee: Payment methods vary by application type
  6. Receive visa: Typically within 3-10 working days

Required Documents

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months
  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport-sized photo (35x45mm)
  • Proof of accommodation in Azerbaijan
  • Return flight ticket
  • Travel insurance (for some nationalities)
  • Bank statement showing sufficient funds

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get an Azerbaijan visa?

E-visas typically take 3 working days, while consular visas may take 5-10 working days depending on the embassy.

What is the cost of an Azerbaijan visa?

E-visas cost $25 + service fee. Consular visa fees vary by country and processing time.

Can I extend my tourist visa in Azerbaijan?

Yes, tourist visas can be extended through the State Migration Service in Azerbaijan.
